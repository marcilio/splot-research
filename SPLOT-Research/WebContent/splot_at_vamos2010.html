SPLOT at VaMoS 2010

We're really excited that several research papers at the Fourth International Workshop on Variability Modelling of Software-intensive Systems (VaMoS 2010) have somehow benefited from SPLOT's online services including SPLOT's Feature Model Editor and the Feature Model Repository. In the last few months, we have observed a growing number of users adding new entries to the repository voluntarily which seems to show that researchers and practitioners need not only tools but also appropriate means to share their models with their peers.

VaMoS 2010 Proceedings can be downloaded here.

In the following, we list the VaMoS 2010 papers that mentioned SPLOT:

  • S. She, R. Lotufo, T. Berger, A. Wasowski, K. Czarnecki: The Variability Model of The Linux Kernel
  • Q. Boucher, A. Classen, P. Faber, P. Heymans: Introducing TVL, a Text-based Feature Modelling Language
  • C. Gauthier, A. Classen, Q. Boucher, P. Heymans, M. Storey, M. Mendonca: XToF - A Tool for Tag-based Product Line Implementation
  • W. Chae, T. Hinrichs: SmartForm: A Web-based Feature Configuration Tool

 

Computer Systems Group / Generative Software Development Lab, University of Waterloo, Canada, 2009-2010.
